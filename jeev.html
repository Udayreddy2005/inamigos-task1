About Project Jeev

Project Jeev is dedicated to promoting animal welfare and compassionate care for stray and abandoned animals. Through rescue operations, feeding drives, awareness campaigns and community support, the initiative helps improve the lives of animals while encouraging kindness and responsibility among people.
