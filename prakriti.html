About Project Prakriti

Project Prakriti is dedicated to promoting environmental sustainability and creating greener communities. Through tree plantation drives, awareness campaigns, clean-up initiatives and advocacy for sustainable practices, the project encourages individuals to take responsibility for protecting the environment. Prakriti inspires communities to contribute towards a healthier and greener future.
