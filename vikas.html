About Project Vikas

Project Vikas is focused on enhancing youth employability and preparing students for successful careers. The initiative offers internship programs, skill development workshops, industry exposure and practical learning opportunities in areas such as AI, Data Analytics, Digital Marketing, Finance and Technology. Vikas helps students bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world experience.
