About Project SEVA

Project SEVA is one of the flagship initiatives of InAmigos Foundation. It focuses on helping underprivileged communities by providing food, groceries, clothing kits, and essential support. The project works to reduce hunger, improve living conditions, and ensure that families receive basic necessities during difficult times.
