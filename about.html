About InAmigos Foundation

Creating sustainable social impact through education, empowerment, welfare and community development.

Who We Are

The InAmigos Foundation is a registered non-profit and CSR-1 certified organization headquartered in Chhattisgarh, India. The organization works across the country to address critical social challenges through community-driven initiatives focused on education, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare and youth development.

Our Mission

📚

Improve access to quality education.

🚀

Empower women and youth through skills and opportunities.

🌱

Promote sustainable environmental practices.

🐾

Protect and care for animals.

Our Vision

To build an inclusive, compassionate, and self-reliant society where every individual has access to opportunities, resources, education and support needed to thrive.
